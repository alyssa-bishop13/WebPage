Preacher's Daughter

Ethel Cain


$59.99

Containing 13 songs with over an hour of playtime!

Preacher's Daughter - praised for it’s storytelling and songwriting, the debut album of singer-songwriter Ethel Cain, released in 2022, by her independent record label Daughters of Cain. The album was entirely produced by Ethel Cain, otherwise known as Hayden Silas Anhedönia.

Preacher's Daughter is a concept album that creates a fictional story centered and inspired by the artist's personal life. It explores intense themes of womanhood, generational trauma, and experiments with beautiful sonics.

Genres explored: Americana, folk, ethereal wave, goth-pop, southern gothic, dark ambient
