About Us

Welcome to Alisa's Vinyl Store, a small independent business owned by ‘Alyssa Bishop’ dedicated to curating a unique selection of music from all over the world! Estabilished in 2025, inspired by ever-changing music, new genres, and of course, the old classics. Ranging from timeless indie american artists, to top 10 Eurovision contestants. We strive to bring the music you love into your home with something you can call yours!

We believe that vinyl records are the ultimate music experience, delivering raw, authentic sound. The large size of vinyls creates a visual masterpiece of iconic art pieces that make a collection a great display!

Alisa's Vinyl Store offers a range of products and services including buying and selling records of all kinds. We also provide custom posters, turntables, and we can special order vinyls just for you. Just use our contact form below for inquires.So join us in celebration of the art of music in it’s most authentic form and begin your journey!
