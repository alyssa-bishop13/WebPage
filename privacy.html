Privacy Policy


At Alisa's Records we value your privacy. The privacy policy outlines what we collect and why when you visit our website, or make a purchase through our online store.


1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you provide directly, such as when you provide personal information in order to make purchases, contact customer support. This can include your name, email address, shipping and billing information, phone number, and any payment methods you share with us. We also collect non-personal data through the use of cookies to improve your experience by personalizing the user expirience to you.

2. How We Use It

We use all information collected to process and fulfill orders, provide support, and communicate with you. We may use this data for several reasons including authenticating your account, contacting you, and fufilling orders. We may use your email address to send promotional offers, new items, and updates, which you may opt out of at anytime.

3. Sharing

We do not share, sell, or trade your personal information to third parties unless required by law, or to fulfill purchases with third party shippers.

4. Third Party Links

Our website is not responsible for the content of external sites, that have their own privacy policy to follow

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our privacy policy please contact us at @ or by using the contact form under About Us.
